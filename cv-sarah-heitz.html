Carnault
2021 – heute
Graphic Designer · Basel

Three brothers, one cigarette, and the entire Black Forest. Carnault is an electronic cigarette, developed and largely manufactured in Switzerland. It's a shared dream that we've continually expanded, refreshed, revitalized, and fueled over the past years. I joined the crew in 2021, when the ship had already been sailing for a good 10 years, just in time for the first working prototypes. Whether it's webshops, physical stores, or the design for the products surrounding the cigarette, in a startup, diverse collaboration and a strong team are essential!

Tacheles
2018 – 2019
Protagonist · Berlin

Please excuse the image quality. It’s emotionally true. In 2018, I was working on my bachelor’s thesis, creating concept art for «Als Gott Schlief», a video game project by Yaar. At the same time, a Berlin-based studio was filming the documentary «Endlich Tacheles» about him, his family, their history, and how a video game might me an outlet for coping with it. Because of how closely I was involved with the project, I became part of the film. Designing characters and scenes for a game about memory and inherited trauma while the documentary showed the people carrying it.

https://endlich-tacheles.org/
